How To Play

Brief Intro

Every run starts from a seeded 52-card deck. Your goal is simple and brutal: predict whether each next card is higher or lower than the current one, all the way to the end.

Guide

  1. Press Click To Play and start a run.
  2. You will see a current face-up card and the next card face-down.
  3. Choose HIGHER or LOWER for the next card.
  4. Equal value cards count as safe and your run continues.
  5. Build streaks to earn cheats; use them at the right moment.
  6. If you clear all 52 cards, your seed can be recorded on the heroes board.

Nudges & Cheats

  • Nudges: Use the up/down arrows beside the current card to temporarily shift its judged value before your next guess.
  • Temporary values: If a card is modified, it is marked as temporary and the run log uses the judged value for that comparison.
  • Cheats in Hand: Tap a cheat card to activate it. Some stack, some are one-shot, and timing is everything.
  • On mobile: Swipe the cheat row to view more cheats, then tap to use.
