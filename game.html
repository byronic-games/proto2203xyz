How To Play

The Goal

Every run starts from a seeded 52-card deck. Your job is simple and brutal: decide whether the next card is higher or lower than the current one, and survive all the way to the end.

The Rules

  • Pick Higher or Lower for the next face-down card.
  • Equal values are safe, so your run continues on a match. Aces are usually low unless a power changes that.
  • A wrong guess ends the run immediately.
  • Blue Deck can award Nudges on correct guesses. Red Deck does not award Nudges, but the next card back shows live history for that face-up card.
  • After choosing a deck, choose that deck's level. Blue Level 1 is the standard pacing, Blue Level 2 slows cheat rewards, and Blue Level 3 keeps that tougher pacing while cutting each cheat offer down to 1 from 2.
  • Clear the full deck to record a winning run on the heroes board.

Powers, Nudges, And Cheats

  • Start Power: Before each run, pick 1 Power from a choice of 2.
  • Blue Deck Nudges: In Blue, every correct Higher guess adds an up nudge and every correct Lower guess adds a down nudge.
  • Nudges: Use the up/down buttons beside the current card to temporarily change its judged value before your next guess.
  • Cheats: Build streaks to earn Cheats. Some cheats act instantly, while others arm protection or a delayed reward for the next card or next few correct guesses.
  • Quick Fingers: This power speeds up Cheat rewards for the level you are playing.
  • Daily: The Daily gives everyone the same seeded deck, opening powers, and cheat offers for that date, and currently always plays as Blue Deck Level 1.
  • Power Reminder: Hold the deck / level / starting power text in the top bar during a run to reread your active power.
  • On mobile: Swipe the cheat row sideways if your cheats run off the screen.

What You See

  • The top HUD shows your current deck and level, your starting power, best run for that deck and level, cards cleared, and cards remaining.
  • The center play area shows the current card, your nudge counts, and the next face-down card.
  • The tracked grid records which cards you have already seen this run.
