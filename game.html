How To Play

The Goal

Clear the full 52-card deck by guessing whether the next card is higher or lower than the current one.

The Basics

  • Equal value cards are safe and your run continues.
  • Every correct guess grows your streak and can earn Cheats.
  • Nudges let you temporarily shift the judged value of the current card before you guess.
  • Some runs also start with a Power that changes the rules or your opening tools.

Screen Guide

  • The top HUD shows your seed, best run, cards cleared, and cards remaining.
  • The card buttons beside the current card show your available up and down nudge counts.
  • The Run Tools row shows Cheats in hand. Tap to use, hold on touch to read the description.
  • The tracked grid records which cards you have already seen this run.
